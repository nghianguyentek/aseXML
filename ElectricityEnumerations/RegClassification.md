# RegClassification Enum

## Purpose

Use to indicate whether it is customer Initiated and regulatory timeframes apply, or not.

### Version 1.0 additions

- Customer Initiated
- Malfunction
- New Meter Deployment
- Shared Fuse
- Other

## Values

- `Customer Initiated`
- `Malfunction`
- `New Meter Deployment`
- `Shared Fuse`
- `Other`
